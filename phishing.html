Phishing Awareness

Learn to recognize and avoid phishing scams.

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a common tactic used by cybercriminals to steal sensitive information, such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details. They do this by masquerading as trustworthy entities in electronic communications.

How to Recognize Phishing Scams

Here are some common signs of phishing attempts:

  • Unsolicited emails or messages from unknown senders.
  • Requests for sensitive information or urgent action.
  • Generic greetings instead of personalized ones.
  • Suspicious links or attachments in emails.
  • Poor grammar or spelling mistakes.

How to Avoid Phishing Scams

To protect yourself from phishing attacks, consider the following tips:

  • Always verify the sender's email address.
  • Hover over links to see the actual URL before clicking.
  • Do not provide sensitive information in response to unsolicited requests.
  • Use security software to protect your devices.
  • Stay informed about the latest phishing tactics.
